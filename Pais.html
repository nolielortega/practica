Pais de nacimiento

Bryant Myers was born and grew up in a neighborhood called Loma Alta from Carolina, Puerto Rico, in what he describes as a hazardous environment (the alleys and streets). "Sometimes we did not have breakfast or lunch, my mom could not buy me some sneakers, that made me want to make a lot of money to have my family well, and that my mother and my brother did not miss anything",[4] relates Bryant on an interview with Infobae. Bryant started writing songs in his home while he studied. He later left his studies in order to pursue his music career.
